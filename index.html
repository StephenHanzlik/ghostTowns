CAIC Forecast

Combine the CAIC zone forecasts with SNOTEL weather stations. Find which areas have recieved the most snow lately and compare them to avalanche forecasts

SNOTEL Dashboard

Access extensice charts and graphs that are plugged into the SNOTEL sites. This page provide accurate analysis of observations that are updated daily.

Slope Angle Maps

Use one or all of the embedded maps to plan your next trip. Slope angle overlays allow for basic identification of avalache prone slopes. Satellite view allows for terrain identification
